KAUST Internship (Unified World Model)

Join King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) as a visiting student intern to work with Haozhe Liu, Mingchen Zhuge, Jinjie Mai and Juergen Schmidhuber on an ambitious project to develop a unified world model.

What You’ll Gain:

  • Access to state-of-the-art resources for cutting-edge research.
  • Hands-on experience in foundational model training.
  • Mentorship from Juergen Schmidhuber and a skilled team.
  • Housing and stipend provided.

Requirements:

  • Full-time, in-person commitment at KAUST.
  • Experiences working on generative models, LLM, agentic systems, etc.
  • Excellent teamwork and communication skills.
  • Please submit your resume and a brief cover letter to haozhe.liu@kaust.edu.sa
